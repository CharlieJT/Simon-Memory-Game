Game Information

Buttons:

1. Start - To begin the game, press 'START' & your first sequence will commence. Also, click 'START' to begin a new game when it's game over!

1. Strict - When 'STRICT' mode is activated (green), if you at any point get your sequence wrong, it will be game over. If 'STRICT' mode is not activated (red), then you can keep trying the current sequence over & over until you get it correct & move to the next sequence.

2. Green Button - (D#).

3. Red Button - (G).

4. Yellow Button - (A#).

5. Blue Button - (D#).


Rules:

When you press the 'START' button, the game will begin with a flashing colour of 1 of the 4 coloured pads from the board & will increment by 1 on the number display with each pattern you've followed correctly. Your goal is to repeat the pattern that has been generated by the computer until you get the sequence wrong. This game is a great test of skill & memory so be careful not to make a mistake!
